## 1.2 Logaritmen

**${\log_a b} = x$** => **$a^x = b$**

**${\log_a b} + {\log_a c} = {\log_a b \cdot c}$**

**${\log_a b} - {\log_a c} = {\log_a \frac{b}{c}}$**

**$r \cdot {\log_a b} = {\log_a b^r}$**

**${\log_a b} = \frac{{\log b}}{{\log a}}$**
